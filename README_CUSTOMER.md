## Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Platform

Navigate to **infoacademy.uk** and experience the APEX OS landing page. The platform automatically detects your device capabilities and optimizes performance accordinglyâ€”whether you're on a high-end gaming PC, a premium tablet (120Hz), or a mobile device.

### Step 2: The Terminal Handshake

The APEX Terminal is your gateway to the platform. Complete the identity handshake:

1. **Enter your full name** (2+ characters)
   - This establishes your identity in the system
   - Creates your personalized journey

2. **Enter your email address**
   - Secures your spot in the cohort
   - Enables personalized communications
   - Required for webinar access

3. **Watch the Handshake Animation**
   - Three-phase authentication:
     - Phase 1: Chromatic Aberration (glitch effect)
     - Phase 2: Biometric Scan
     - Phase 3: Player 1 Connected
   - Indicates successful system access

### Step 3: Choose Your Path

After authentication, you'll see two options:

- **Blue Pill (PILL 1)**: Personal Builder Track
- **Red Pill (PILL 2)**: Business Architect Track

Hover over each pill to see a preview of your journey. Click to commit to your path. This choice determines:
- Your learning curriculum
- AI personality (JARVIS adapts to your track)
- Community modules unlocked
- Recommended tools and resources

### Step 4: Unlock Module 00

Upon selection, your terminal updates with:
- Confirmation of your chosen path
- List of unlocked modules
- Access to the command interface
- Invitation to join the next webinar cohort
