### The Adaptation Engine

**Core Algorithm:**

```python
class AdaptiveEngine:
    def analyze_student(self, student_id):
        # Collect interaction data
        interactions = self.get_interactions(student_id)
        
        # Calculate metrics
        pace = self.calculate_pace(interactions)
        comprehension = self.assess_comprehension(interactions)
        engagement = self.measure_engagement(interactions)
        
        # Identify knowledge gaps
        gaps = self.find_knowledge_gaps(interactions)
        
        # Generate personalized path
        path = self.generate_path(pace, comprehension, gaps)
        
        return path
    
    def adapt_content(self, student_id, content):
        path = self.get_student_path(student_id)
        
        # Adjust difficulty
        if path['comprehension'] < 0.7:
            content = self.simplify(content)
        elif path['comprehension'] > 0.9:
            content = self.enrich(content)
        
        # Add relevant examples
        content = self.add_personalized_examples(content, student_id)
        
        # Insert review material for gaps
        content = self.insert_gap_review(content, path['gaps'])
        
        return content
```

**Key Features:**
- Real-time adaptation (updates within seconds)
- Multi-modal analysis (video watch time, code submissions, quiz results)
- Predictive dropout prevention (identifies at-risk students early)
- Content generation (AI creates personalized examples)

### Risk Matrix

|------|------------|--------|------------|---------------|
| AI model costs increase | Medium | High | Multi-provider fallback, optimization | Low |

| Key person dependency | Low | High | Documented processes, team expansion | Low |
| Regulatory changes (AI Act) | Low | Medium | Compliance-first design, legal advisor | Low |
| Curriculum obsolescence | Low | High | Living curriculum, AI updates | Low |
| Platform technical issues | Low | High | Redundancy, monitoring, 99.9% SLA | Low |

### Detailed Risk Analysis

#### Risk 1: AI Model Cost Escalation

**Scenario:** OpenAI/Google increase API prices by 3-5x

**Impact:** Margins compress from 85% to 60%

**Mitigation:**
- Multi-provider architecture (already implemented)
- Cost optimization (caching, batching)
- Price adjustment (pass 50% to customers)
- In-house model fine-tuning (long-term)

**Probability:** Medium (API prices have been stable, but risk exists)

**Residual Risk:** Low

#### Risk 2: Competition from Big Tech

**Scenario:** Coursera/Udemy launch adaptive learning platform

**Impact:** Market share pressure, price competition

**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage (18-month head start)
- Network effects (community stickiness)
- Accelerator pipeline (unique differentiator)
- Premium positioning (not competing on price)

**Probability:** Medium (likely to happen eventually)

**Residual Risk:** Medium (but we have strong moat)

#### Risk 3: Economic Downturn

**Scenario:** Recession reduces discretionary spending on education

**Impact:** Lower conversion rates, slower growth

**Mitigation:**
- Recession-resistant category (upskilling demand increases)
- Payment plans reduce upfront cost
- ROI-focused marketing (salary increases)
- Corporate training (B2B revenue diversification)

**Probability:** Medium (economic cycles)

**Residual Risk:** Low (historically counter-cyclical)

#### Risk 4: Key Person Risk

**Scenario:** Founder or key engineer leaves

**Impact:** Product development slows, investor confidence drops

**Mitigation:**
- Documented processes and code
- Team expansion (not dependent on one person)
- Vesting schedules (4-year vesting)
- Succession planning

**Probability:** Low (strong team, good culture)

**Residual Risk:** Low

#### Risk 5: Regulatory Changes

**Scenario:** EU AI Act restricts AI in education

**Impact:** Compliance costs, feature limitations

**Mitigation:**
- Compliance-first design (already GDPR compliant)
- Legal advisor on retainer
- Transparency in AI usage
- Human oversight built-in

**Probability:** Low (AI Act focuses on high-risk applications)

**Residual Risk:** Low

### Contingency Plans

**Plan B: If Academy Growth Slows**
- Pivot to corporate training (B2B)
- Increase price for premium tier
- Focus on accelerator (higher margin)

**Plan C: If AI Costs Spike**
- Implement usage-based pricing for students
- Optimize AI calls aggressively
- Develop in-house models
